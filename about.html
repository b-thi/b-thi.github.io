Least Angle Regression

Least-Angle Regression (LARS) is an algorithm for fitting linear regression models to high-dimensional data, developed by Bradley Efron, Trevor Hastie, Iain Johnstone and Robert Tibshirani. This project re-preseants the mathematical details outlined in the original paper in a more approachable manner and highlights the algorithms relative superiority through simulations.
